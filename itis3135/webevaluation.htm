Website Evaluations

Typeset Design

Typeset Design is a bad website because it does not follow the CRAP principles.

  • CRAP
  • C-Contrast
  • When looking at this website their is very little contrast that is evident.
  • R-epetition
  • There is lots of repition amongst the fonts, font-styles and background-color. It's difficult for me to tell what's happenning because of this.
  • A-lignment
  • Links are not consistent. Text is not neatly formatted. Overall, it's a mess when it comes to alignment.
  • P-Proximity
  • The page has an excessive amount of whitespace. The website's links are not properly positioned. There was little attention put into where goods were displayed on the website. It is inconvenient for me as a user to go around.

Amazon

Amazon, on the other hand, does an excellent job following the principles of CRAP.

  • CRAP
  • C-Contrast
  • There is a good amount of contrast on the website. Makes it easy for me to navigate Amazon.
  • R-epetition
  • Not a lot of repition. Appropriate and consistent use of repition amongst parts of the website.
  • A-lignment
  • Links are appropriately linked across the website. Can easily see where everything is on a given page.
  • P-Proximity
  • Good amount of whitespace. Enough to where I can see how everything connects. Things are placed appropriately on the website. I can tell someone put thought into the design of Amazon.
